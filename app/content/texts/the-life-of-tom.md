### Chapter 1: Hello! This is Me

Hello. My name is Tom. I am twenty-five years old. I am from the United Kingdom. I live in a big city. The city is London. I am an English teacher. I work in a school. I like my job very much.

I am tall and thin. I have short brown hair. I have blue eyes. I wear glasses. Today, I am wearing a blue shirt and black trousers. I am wearing brown shoes. I am happy today. I am a very happy person. I like to smile. I like to help people.

My birthday is in May. It is on the fifth of May. On my birthday, I eat a big cake. I celebrate with my family and my friends. I have a lot of friends. They are very good people. I like to spend time with them. Life is good. I am young and I have a good life. I want to tell you about my life, my family, and my days.

### Chapter 2: My Family

I have a very nice family. We are a happy family. There are five people in my family. There is my father, my mother, my sister, my brother, and me.

My father’s name is John. He is fifty-five years old. He is a doctor. He works in a hospital. He is very tall. He has grey hair and green eyes. He works hard every day. He likes to read books in the evening.

My mother’s name is Mary. She is fifty-two years old. She is a nurse. She works in the same hospital as my father. She is short and beautiful. She has long blonde hair and blue eyes. She is very kind. She makes very good food. She likes to cook.

My sister’s name is Emma. She is twenty years old. She is a university student. She studies art. She can draw very well. She paints beautiful pictures. She has blonde hair like my mother.

My brother’s name is David. He is twelve years old. He is a young boy. He goes to school. He likes to play football. He plays football every day with his friends. He is very fast. He has brown hair like me. I love my family very much. We eat dinner together every Sunday.

### Chapter 3: My House

I do not live with my parents. I live in a flat. My flat is in the center of London. It is a small flat, but it is very nice. It is my home.

My flat has four rooms. It has a living room, a kitchen, a bedroom, and a bathroom. The living room is big. The walls are white. In the living room, there is a brown sofa. There is a small table. There is a big television on the wall. I watch television in the evening. I have a green plant in the living room. It is next to the window.

The kitchen is small but clean. There is a fridge, a cooker, and a sink. The fridge is white. Inside the fridge, there is milk, water, and food. There is a small round table with two chairs. I eat my breakfast here every morning.

My bedroom is my favorite room. The bed is very comfortable. The bed is big. I have a wardrobe for my clothes. I have a desk and a chair. I have a computer on my desk. I read books in my bed.

The bathroom is very small. There is a shower, a toilet, and a mirror. I wash my face in the mirror every morning. I like my flat. It is warm and quiet.

### Chapter 4: My Daily Routine

I have a routine. Every day from Monday to Friday is the same. I wake up early. I wake up at exactly seven o'clock in the morning. I get out of bed. I go to the bathroom. I take a shower. I wash my hair. I brush my teeth. I get dressed. I put on my clothes for work.

At half past seven, I go to the kitchen. I make breakfast. I eat breakfast. After breakfast, I drink a cup of coffee. I need coffee in the morning.

At eight o'clock, I leave my flat. I walk to the bus stop. I wait for the bus. The bus comes at ten past eight. I take the bus to school. The journey is thirty minutes long. I arrive at the school at a quarter to nine.

I start work at nine o'clock. I teach English to children. The children are very good. I work from nine o'clock in the morning to four o'clock in the afternoon. At twelve o'clock, I have lunch. I eat lunch with the other teachers in the school.

At four o'clock, I finish work. I go home. I take the bus again. I arrive home at five o'clock. I relax. I listen to music. I make dinner at seven o'clock. After dinner, I watch a movie or read a book. At ten o'clock, I go to the bathroom. I brush my teeth again. I go to bed. I sleep very well.

### Chapter 5: Food and Drink

I love food. I like to eat healthy food, but sometimes I eat unhealthy food.  
For breakfast, I usually eat toast with butter and jam. Sometimes, I eat an egg. I always drink black coffee. I do not put sugar or milk in my coffee. I like water. I drink a lot of water every day.

For lunch, I eat a sandwich. I make my sandwich at home. It has cheese, tomato, and lettuce. Sometimes I put chicken in my sandwich. I also eat an apple or a banana. Fruit is very good for you. It gives you energy.

For dinner, I like to cook hot food. I like pasta. Pasta is my favorite food. I cook pasta with tomato sauce and vegetables. I like carrots, onions, and potatoes. Sometimes I eat meat. I eat fish on Fridays. I like fish with rice. I do not like mushrooms. Mushrooms are bad. I do not eat them.

On Saturdays, I do not cook. I go to a restaurant with my friends. We eat pizza. I love pizza with extra cheese. We drink orange juice or cola. After pizza, we eat dessert. I like chocolate ice cream. Food makes me very happy.

### Chapter 6: My City

London is a very big city. It is in England. Many people live here. There are many tall buildings. There are many cars, buses, and taxis. The taxis in London are black. The buses are red. The red buses are very famous.

In my city, there are many interesting places. There is a big river. The river is the River Thames. There are many bridges over the river. There are big parks. I like to go to the park on Saturday morning. I walk under the trees. I see birds and dogs. People sit on the grass. The grass is very green.

There are many shops in London. You can buy clothes, food, and books. There are big supermarkets. There are hospitals, schools, and police stations. There are many museums. The museums are free. I like to look at old things in the museum.

The weather in my city is often bad. It rains a lot. It is often cloudy. But sometimes the sun shines. When it is sunny, the city is beautiful. Everyone goes outside. I love London. It is a busy city, but it is a great city.

### Chapter 7: My Best Friend

My best friend is Mark. Mark is twenty-six years old. He is one year older than me. We are friends from school. We know each other for a long time.

Mark is a mechanic. He works in a garage. He fixes cars. He is very good at his job. He loves cars. He has a red sports car. He drives very fast, but he is a safe driver.

Mark is a big man. He is taller than me. He has short black hair and brown eyes. He has a beard. He is very strong. He goes to the gym every day. He lifts heavy weights.

Mark is a very funny person. He always makes me laugh. He tells good jokes. We do many things together. On the weekend, we go to the cinema. We watch action movies. We like the same movies. We also play video games at his house. His house is near my flat.

Mark is not married. He does not have a wife. He does not have children. He has a dog. We walk his dog in the park. Mark is a very good friend. I can talk to him about my problems. He always listens. He always helps me.

### Chapter 8: Hobbies and Free Time

I have a lot of free time on the weekend. I do not work on Saturday and Sunday. I have many hobbies. A hobby is something you do for fun.

My first hobby is reading. I read a lot of books. I read fiction and non-fiction. I like stories about history. I buy books from the bookshop. I have a big shelf in my bedroom. It is full of books.

My second hobby is sports. I play tennis. I play tennis on Sunday morning. I play with my friend Mark. We play in the park. I am not a great player, but I try my best. Tennis is very good exercise. I run a lot. It makes me tired but happy.

My third hobby is music. I can play the guitar. I have a brown acoustic guitar. I play it in my living room. I sing songs. I am not a good singer, but I enjoy it. I listen to music on my phone. I like pop music and rock music. I do not like classical music. It is slow for me.

Sometimes, my hobby is just sleeping. When I am very tired, I sleep for ten hours. Sleep is very important for the body and the mind.

### Chapter 9: Animals and Pets

I like animals. Animals are beautiful. I do not have a pet in my flat. My flat is too small for a pet. But I really want a pet. I want a cat. Cats are quiet and clean. They sleep a lot. They are very soft.

My parents have a dog. His name is Max. Max is a golden retriever. He is a big dog. He is yellow. He is five years old. Max is very friendly. When I visit my parents, Max runs to the door. He jumps and barks. He wags his tail. He is happy to see me. I throw a ball, and Max brings it back to me. He loves to play. He eats meat and dog biscuits. He drinks water from a big bowl.

I also like wild animals. I go to the zoo sometimes. The zoo is a place with many wild animals. At the zoo, I see lions, tigers, and elephants. Elephants are very big. They have big ears and a long trunk. I see monkeys. Monkeys are very funny. They jump from tree to tree. They eat bananas. I see giraffes. Giraffes are very tall. They have long necks. They eat leaves from the trees. Animals are wonderful.

### Chapter 10: Seasons and Weather

There are four seasons in a year. The seasons are spring, summer, autumn, and winter.

Spring is a beautiful season. It starts in March. The weather gets warm. The sun shines more. The flowers start to grow. The trees are green again. I like to walk in the park in spring. I listen to the birds singing.

Summer is my favorite season. It starts in June. The weather is hot. Sometimes it is very hot. The days are very long. The sun goes down late at night. In summer, people wear light clothes. I wear a t-shirt, shorts, and sandals. In summer, people go to the beach. They swim in the sea. They eat ice cream.

Autumn starts in September. The weather gets cool. The leaves on the trees change color. They turn red, yellow, and brown. Then, they fall to the ground. It is windy in autumn. Sometimes it rains. I wear a light jacket in autumn. I go back to school to teach.

Winter starts in December. Winter is very cold. The days are short. It gets dark early in the afternoon. Sometimes, it snows. The snow is white and cold. Children love the snow. They make snowmen. In winter, I wear warm clothes. I wear a thick coat, a scarf, a hat, and gloves. I stay inside my warm flat. I drink hot tea.

### Chapter 11: Shopping and Money

I go shopping every week. I go to the supermarket on Saturday afternoon. I take a big bag with me. I push a trolley in the supermarket. I buy food for the week. I buy bread, milk, eggs, cheese, meat, and vegetables. I also buy soap and toilet paper.

When I finish, I go to the checkout. I talk to the cashier. The cashier says, "Hello." I say, "Hello." The cashier scans my items. I have to pay for my shopping. The cashier tells me the price. "That is forty-five pounds, please." I open my wallet. I do not pay with cash. I do not have coins or banknotes. I pay with my credit card. I tap my card on the machine. It is very fast. The cashier gives me a receipt. I say, "Thank you. Goodbye."

Sometimes, I buy clothes. I go to a clothes shop in the city center. I need a new shirt. I look at the shirts. I find a nice blue shirt. I look at the size. It is a medium. That is my size. I look at the price tag. It is twenty pounds. That is cheap. I go to the fitting room. I try the shirt on. It looks good. I buy the shirt. I am happy with my new shirt.

### Chapter 12: A Trip to the Beach

Today is a special day. It is Saturday in the summer. It is very hot. The sun is shining in the blue sky. I am not in London today. I am at the beach with my friend Mark.

We wake up very early. We wake up at six o'clock. Mark drives his red car. We drive for two hours. We listen to loud music in the car. We sing along. We arrive at the beach at eight o'clock.

The beach is beautiful. The sand is yellow and soft. The sea is blue. We find a good place. We put our towels on the sand. We take off our shirts. We put on sun cream. The sun is very strong. We do not want to burn.

We run into the water. The water is cold at first, but then it is nice. We swim for a long time. We jump in the waves. We laugh a lot.

At one o'clock, we are hungry. We go to a small restaurant near the beach. We eat fish and chips. Fish and chips is a very traditional British food. It is delicious. We drink cold lemonade.

In the afternoon, we walk on the beach. We collect some beautiful shells. We sit on the sand and talk. We watch the sun go down. The sky turns pink and orange. It is a perfect day. We drive home. We are very tired, but very happy.

### Chapter 13: Health and the Body

It is very important to be healthy. I try to be healthy. I eat good food and I do exercise. I have a healthy body. Let me describe the human body.

I have a head. On my head, I have hair. I have a face. On my face, I have two eyes to see. I have a nose to smell. I have two ears to hear. I have a mouth to eat and to speak. Inside my mouth, I have white teeth and a pink tongue.

I have a neck. I have two shoulders. I have two arms. At the end of my arms, I have two hands. I have five fingers on my left hand and five fingers on my right hand. I have ten fingers in total. I use my hands to write, to cook, and to play the guitar.

I have a chest and a stomach. I have two legs. My legs are long. I use my legs to walk and run. At the end of my legs, I have two feet. I have ten toes.

Sometimes, I am not healthy. Sometimes, I am sick. Last month, I had a cold. I had a bad cough. My head hurt. I had a headache. I was very hot. I had a fever. I went to the doctor. The doctor checked my throat. The doctor gave me medicine. I stayed in bed for three days. I drank hot water with lemon and honey. I rested. After three days, I felt better. Now, I am strong again.

### Chapter 14: Technology and Phones

We live in a modern world. Technology is everywhere. I use technology every day. I have a smartphone. My phone is black and very thin. It has a big screen. I carry my phone in my pocket.

I use my phone for many things. I use it to make phone calls. I call my mother every evening. I say, "Hello Mom, how are you?" We talk for ten minutes. I also use my phone to send text messages. I message my friends. We use emojis. Emojis are little pictures of happy faces or sad faces.

I use my phone to take photos. The camera on my phone is very good. I take photos of my family, my food, and beautiful places. I put the photos on the internet. I share them with my friends.

I also have a laptop computer at home. A laptop is a computer you can carry. I use my laptop for my work. I write lessons for my students. I use the internet to find information. The internet is very big. You can find anything on the internet. I watch videos on the internet. I read the news.

Technology is very useful. It makes life easy. But sometimes, it is too much. Sometimes, I turn off my phone. I close my laptop. I just sit in silence. I read a paper book. It is good to have a break from screens.

### Chapter 15: The Future

We are at the end of my story. You know a lot about me now. You know my name, my age, my family, and my friends. You know about my flat, my job, and my hobbies.

What about the future? What am I going to do next?

Next year, I am going to travel. I want to visit Spain. I want to go to Madrid and Barcelona. I am learning Spanish now. I know a few words. I can say "Hola" and "Gracias". Spanish is a beautiful language. I want to eat Spanish food. I want to see the beautiful buildings.

In the future, I want to buy a house. I do not want to live in a flat forever. I want a house with a garden. I want to grow flowers and vegetables in my garden. I want to get a dog. I will call him Buster.

I also want to continue my job as a teacher. I want to help more children learn English. English is an important language. People speak English all over the world.

I am very excited about the future. I think it is going to be great. Thank you for reading my story. Thank you for practicing your English with me. You are doing a very good job. Keep reading, keep listening, and keep speaking. English is fun! Have a wonderful day. Goodbye!
