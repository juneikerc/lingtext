Last weekend, my family and I went to the mountains. The trees were green and the air was fresh. We stayed in a small wooden cabin. It had two bedrooms and a small kitchen.

In the morning, we woke up early and walked in the forest. We saw many birds. Some were red and some were blue. My father took photos of the birds and the trees.

We walked for two hours and saw a small river. The water was cold and clear. We sat near the river and rested. It was quiet, and there was no city noise.

For lunch, my father cooked on a fire. We ate sandwiches and apples. After lunch, we walked to a small hill. From the top, we saw the valley, a lake, and other cabins.

In the afternoon, we went back to the cabin. My sister and I played cards. My mother read a book. In the evening, it was cold, so we stayed inside and talked.

At night, we looked at the stars. The sky was dark and full of stars. We also saw the moon. Then we went to sleep and rested well.

On Sunday, it rained a little. We ate eggs, bread, and orange juice. Then we went for one last walk. We saw a squirrel in a tree.

At 2 o'clock, we went home. We packed our bags and cleaned the cabin. I was sad to leave, but happy to go home. I want to go back next summer.
