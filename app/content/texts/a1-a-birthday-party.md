# A Birthday Party

**Lucas:** Hello, Emma! Happy birthday!

**Emma:** Thank you, Lucas! I am happy you can come.

**Lucas:** I have a gift for you. Here you are.

**Emma:** Oh, thank you! Can I open it now?

**Lucas:** Yes, of course!

Emma opens the box. Inside, she finds a red hat.

**Emma:** Wow! I love this hat.

**Lucas:** I am glad you like it. Let us go to the garden.

They walk outside. Many people are sitting at tables.

**Emma:** This is my friend Lucas, everyone!

**Everyone:** Hello, Lucas!

Emma's mother comes near.

**Mrs. Brown:** Hello! Please, have some food.

**Lucas:** Thank you. I am hungry.

Lucas goes to the food table. He sees sandwiches and a big chocolate cake.

**Emma:** Do you want some cake?

**Lucas:** Yes, please! It looks very good.

Emma gives Lucas a piece of cake.

**Lucas:** Mmm! This is the best cake ever!

Music starts to play.

**Lucas:** Do you want to dance?

**Emma:** I do not dance well.

**Lucas:** That is okay. We can try together.

They dance slowly. The sun is warm.

**Emma:** This is the best birthday ever!

Later, Emma opens all her gifts.

**Emma:** I have the best friends.

**Everyone:** Happy birthday, Emma!

**Lucas:** I should go now.

**Emma:** Thank you for coming. See you tomorrow?

**Lucas:** Yes, see you tomorrow!

Emma waves goodbye. She is very happy. It was a perfect party.
