# A Visit to the Doctor

**Sarah:** Mom, I do not feel well.

**Mom:** What is wrong?

**Sarah:** My head hurts. And I feel cold.

**Mom:** Let me check.

Mom puts her hand on Sarah's forehead.

**Mom:** You have a fever. We need to go to the doctor.

**Sarah:** Do I have to?

**Mom:** The doctor will help you. Do not worry.

They get in the car. They arrive in five minutes.

**Mom:** Let us go inside.

The waiting room is clean and bright.

**Receptionist:** Good morning. How can I help you?

**Mom:** My daughter has an appointment with Dr. Smith.

**Receptionist:** Please sit down.

A nurse comes out.

**Nurse:** Sarah? The doctor is ready.

The doctor smiles at Sarah.

**Dr. Smith:** Hello, Sarah! How are you?

**Sarah:** Not good. My head hurts.

**Dr. Smith:** Open your mouth, please.

The doctor looks inside with a small light.

**Dr. Smith:** Now, breathe in and out.

**Dr. Smith:** You have a small cold. Nothing serious.

**Sarah:** Will I get better soon?

**Dr. Smith:** Yes. You need rest and water.

**Dr. Smith:** Stay home tomorrow. Sleep well.

**Sarah:** Thank you, Doctor.

At home, Sarah lies down on her bed.

**Mom:** Take this medicine.

**Sarah:** Thank you, Mom.

Sarah closes her eyes. She will feel better tomorrow.
