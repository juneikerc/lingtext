---
title: "My First Letter to a Friend"
level: "a1"
date: "2026-04-22"
---

Today I write a letter to my friend Tom. He lives in another city. I miss him very much.

I take a pen and some paper. I sit at my desk near the window. The sun is shining. I feel happy.

"Dear Tom," I write. "How are you? I am fine. I think about you often."

I tell Tom about my week. I write about my new job. I write about my sister's birthday party. I tell him that my dog is big now.

"I want to see you soon," I write. "Can you come to my house next month? We can watch movies together. We can eat pizza. It will be fun."

I read the letter again. I want to be sure it is good. I put the letter in an envelope. I write Tom's address on the front.

Tomorrow I will go to the post office. I will buy a stamp. I will send the letter to Tom.

I hope Tom smiles when he reads my letter. Good friends are important. I am lucky to have Tom.
