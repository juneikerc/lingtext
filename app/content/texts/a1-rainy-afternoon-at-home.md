title: "Rainy Afternoon at Home"
level: "a1"
date: "2026-04-08"
---

Today, the weather is rainy and cold. I stay at home all afternoon. I look out the window and see gray clouds and wet streets. I do not go outside.

In my room, I sit on the sofa with a blanket. I drink hot tea from my favorite cup. Then, I read a short story. The story is about a boy and his cat. It is simple but nice.

At 3:00, my mother makes soup in the kitchen. The house smells very good. I help her with the bowls and spoons. We eat the soup together and talk about our day.

Later, I listen to music and write in my notebook. I write about my friends, my family, and my plans for tomorrow. The rain falls softly, and I feel calm.

In the evening, I watch one episode of a funny TV show with my brother. We laugh a lot.

I sometimes like rainy days. They are quiet, warm, and peaceful.
