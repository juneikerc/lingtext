# The Lost Key - Chapter 1: A Bad Morning

"**Wait**," she **said**, **feeling** **worried**. "I **can't find** my **keys**!"

"Maria, I **think** I **lost** my apartment **keys**!" Ana **said**, her **face** turning **white**. "What am I going to **do**? My **roommate** is **away** for the weekend!"
