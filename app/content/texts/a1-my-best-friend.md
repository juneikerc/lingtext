level: "a1"
date: "2026-05-08"
---

My name is Tom. I am ten years old. I live in a small house with my mother and my father. I go to school every day. I like school because I can see my best friend.

His name is Ben. Ben is ten years old too. He lives near my house. We walk to school together every morning. Ben has brown hair and blue eyes. He is tall and kind. Everyone likes him because he smiles a lot.

Ben and I sit together in class. We share our books and our pens. When I do not understand something, Ben helps me. When Ben forgets his lunch, I share my food with him. We are a good team.

After school, we play in the park near our houses. Sometimes we play football. Sometimes we run or jump. When it rains, we stay inside and play games. Ben always wins, but I do not care. We have fun together.

On Saturdays, Ben comes to my house. My mother makes sandwiches and juice for us. We eat in the garden and talk about our week. Ben likes my mother's food very much. He says it is the best food in town.

Ben has a small dog. The dog's name is Max. Max is black and white. He runs fast and barks loud. When we walk in the park, Max comes with us. I like Max because he is funny and friendly.

I am happy because I have a good friend. Ben is kind, funny, and smart. We help each other every day. I think friends are very important. I like Ben, and I know he likes me too.
