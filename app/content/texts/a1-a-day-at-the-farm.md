# A Day at the Farm

Tom and his mother go to a small farm. It is Sunday morning. The sun is bright and warm.

**Tom:** Look, Mom! A big cow!

**Mom:** Yes, Tom. The cow is eating grass.

Tom runs to the fence. He sees three cows. They are brown and white.

**Tom:** Can I touch it?

**Mom:** No, Tom. We look. We do not touch.

Tom nods. They walk to the next area. There are many chickens. They walk around and eat seeds.

**Tom:** The chickens are small!

**Mom:** Yes. And they make eggs.

A chicken walks near Tom. Tom smiles. He does not touch it.

**Tom:** Hello, chicken!

They walk more. Tom sees a big horse. The horse is tall and strong.

**Tom:** Wow! The horse is very big!

**Mom:** Yes. The farmer rides the horse.

A farmer comes near. He has a red hat.

**Farmer:** Hello, little boy! Do you like my horse?

**Tom:** Yes! It is beautiful.

The farmer smiles. He gives Tom a small piece of apple.

**Farmer:** You can give this to the horse.

**Tom:** Really?

**Mom:** Go ahead, Tom. But be gentle.

Tom walks slowly. He gives the apple to the horse. The horse eats it.

**Tom:** He likes it!

**Farmer:** He likes apples very much.

They walk to the pig area. The pigs are pink and fat. They sleep in the mud.

**Tom:** They are funny.

**Mom:** Pigs like mud. It keeps them cool.

Tom sees a baby pig. It is very small.

**Tom:** The baby pig is so little!

**Mom:** Yes. All animals have babies.

They sit on a bench. Tom drinks some water. His mother gives him a sandwich.

**Tom:** This is a good day.

**Mom:** Yes. Farms are nice places.

They see some rabbits in a small garden. The rabbits have long ears. They jump very fast.

**Tom:** I like the rabbits!

**Mom:** They are fast, yes.

Later, the farmer shows them a big barn. Inside, there are many bales of hay.

**Tom:** It smells good here.

**Farmer:** That is the smell of hay.

Tom waves goodbye to all the animals. He is very happy.

**Tom:** Can we come again?

**Mom:** Yes, Tom. We can come next month.

**Tom:** Great!

Tom holds his mother's hand. They walk to the car. It was a perfect day at the farm.
