My desk is in my room. It is small, but I like it. I sit at my desk every day. I read, write, and draw there.

On my desk, I have a book, a pen, and a blue cup. The cup has pencils in it. I also have a small lamp. The lamp is white. It helps me see at night.

My desk is not always clean. Sometimes I have many papers on it. Sometimes my books are on the chair. Then I cannot sit down.

On Sunday, I clean my desk. First, I put my books on the shelf. Then I put my pens in the cup. I throw old papers in the bin. I clean the table with a cloth.

Now my desk looks good. I can find my pen. I can find my notebook. I feel happy because my room is quiet and clean.

Do you have a desk? Is it clean or messy? A clean desk helps me study, and it helps me feel calm.
