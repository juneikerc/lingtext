---
title: "My New Bicycle"
level: "a2"
date: "2026-04-08"
---

Last month, I got a new bicycle for my birthday. It is blue and white, and it has a small basket in the front. The seat is black, and the wheels are very strong. I was very happy because I wanted a bicycle for a long time. Every time I saw other people riding in the park, I thought, “I want to do that too.” When I opened my birthday gift and saw the bicycle, I smiled very big. My parents laughed because I was so excited.

My father helped me check the bicycle in the afternoon. He looked at the wheels, the basket, and the brakes. He also showed me how to change the seat because at first it was a little too high for me. After that, I cleaned the basket and put a small bottle of water inside. I wanted to ride it the same day, but it started to rain, so I had to wait until Saturday.

On Saturday morning, I went to the park with my brother. The weather was sunny and warm, and the sky was very blue. There were many people in the park. Some people were running, some were walking their dogs, and some children were playing with a ball. At first, I was a little nervous because the bicycle was new, but after ten minutes, I felt better. My brother rode next to me and told me to go slowly. We rode on a small path near the trees and the lake.

We stopped two times. The first time, we drank some water and stood under a big tree for a few minutes. I looked at the lake and saw ducks swimming in the water. The second time, we sat on a bench and watched the ducks. We also saw a man with a camera taking pictures of birds. My brother said he wanted to learn photography one day. I told him I wanted to ride my bicycle to the park every weekend.

Later, we rode to a small café near the park entrance. We did not go inside, but we bought two cold drinks and sat outside. I put my bicycle next to the table and looked at it many times because I liked it so much. My brother said, “You look very proud,” and I said, “Yes, because this is my first new bicycle.”

Then, we rode back home. On the way home, I felt stronger and more comfortable. I was tired, but I also felt proud and excited. When we arrived, my mother asked, “How was your ride?” I told her everything about the lake, the ducks, and the café. In the evening, I cleaned my bicycle again and put it in a safe place.

Now, I use my bicycle every weekend. Sometimes I go to the park, and sometimes I ride around my neighborhood. It helps me exercise, and it also makes me feel free. I think a bicycle is not only a gift. For me, it is also the start of a new hobby that makes me happy.
