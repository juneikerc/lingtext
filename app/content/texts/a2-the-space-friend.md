Tom is an astronaut in a very big spaceship. He travels in space and looks at the stars every night from a small window. One day, his computer makes a loud noise and the lights turn red. Tom is a little bit scared because the ship stops moving near a giant purple planet. He puts on his space suit and opens the door to see the stars.

The planet has three small moons and the sky is beautiful. Suddenly, Tom sees a friendly robot on a rock. The robot has big yellow eyes and it waves its metal hand. The robot helps Tom with the computer and the spaceship works again. Tom says "Thank you" and gives the robot a silver pen as a gift.

After the repair, Tom and the robot sit together on the purple rock. The robot shows Tom a map of the galaxy. They eat some space food and drink cold water. Even though they speak different languages, they are good friends. Tom feels very lucky to meet such a special person in the middle of the dark universe.

When it is time to leave, the robot gives Tom a small, glowing stone. The stone is green and very warm. Tom puts the stone in a box to keep it safe. He waves goodbye to the robot and starts the engine of his big spaceship. The robot watches the ship go up into the black sky.

Now, Tom is back on Earth with his family. He tells his children the story of the purple planet and the kind robot. Every night, he looks at the sky and remembers his friend in the stars. He wants to return to space one day to explore new worlds and see the yellow eyes of his friend again.
