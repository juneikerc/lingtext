title: "My Summer Holiday"
level: "a2"
date: "2026-05-08"
---

Last summer, I went on holiday with my family. We visited a small town near the sea. It was my first time there, and I was very excited. The town was quiet and beautiful, with white houses and narrow streets.

We traveled by car. The journey took four hours, but it was not boring. I listened to music and looked out the window. My little brother asked many questions during the trip. He wanted to know how long the journey would take and what we would eat for lunch. My mother answered all his questions with patience. My sister slept most of the time because she gets tired in cars. My father drove carefully, and my mother told us stories about her childhood.

When we arrived, the sun was shining and the air smelled of salt and flowers. Our apartment was small but comfortable. It had two bedrooms, a kitchen, and a balcony with a view of the sea. I chose the smaller bedroom because I liked the blue walls.

On the first day, we walked around the town. We visited a small market where local people sold fresh fruit and handmade clothes. I bought a blue shirt for my friend. It was not expensive, and the seller was very kind. My mother bought some cheese and bread for dinner. After the market, we sat in a small café and drank cold lemonade. The lemonade was very fresh, and it helped us forget the heat.

The next day, we went to the beach. The water was warmer than I expected, and the sand was soft and clean. We swam in the morning and then ate lunch at a small restaurant near the sea. I tried fish for the first time. It was good, but my sister did not like it. She ate pizza instead.

In the evening, we often walked along the beach. We watched the sunset and talked about our plans for the next day. My father wanted to visit an old castle, and my mother preferred to go shopping. We decided to do both.

On Wednesday, we visited the old castle. It was bigger than I imagined, and the walls were very old. A guide told us stories about the people who lived there many years ago. I liked the garden most because it was full of colorful flowers.

On our last day, we climbed a small hill near the town. From the top, we could see the whole sea. It was the most beautiful view I have ever seen. We took many pictures and laughed a lot. I felt sad to leave, but I was also happy because we had a wonderful time.

This summer, I want to go back to the same town. I hope my family can come too. I will bring more money so I can buy more gifts for my friends. Holidays are important because they help us relax and spend time together.
