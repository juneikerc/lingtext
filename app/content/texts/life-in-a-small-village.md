---
title: "Life in a Small Village"
level: "a1"
---

I live in a small village. My village is in the countryside. It is a very quiet and beautiful place. There are many trees and flowers everywhere. The air is fresh and clean. In my village, everyone knows each other. When I walk down the street, I say hello to my neighbors. They are very friendly and kind. We help each other when someone has a problem.

In the center of the village, there is a small square. In the square, there is a big old tree. Under the tree, there are some benches. Older people like to sit there and talk. They talk about the weather and their gardens. There is also a small shop in the square. The shop sells bread, milk, eggs, and many other things. The shopkeeper is Mr. Brown. He is very old and always has a smile on his face. I go to his shop every morning to buy fresh bread for my family.

My house is near the forest. It is a small house with a red roof. I have a big garden behind my house. In my garden, I grow vegetables like carrots, tomatoes, and potatoes. I also have some fruit trees. I have an apple tree and a pear tree. In the summer, the fruit is very sweet. I like to eat apples from my tree. My mother makes delicious jam from the fruit. My father helps me in the garden. We work together on the weekends.

Every morning, I wake up early. I hear the birds singing in the trees. I open my window and breathe the fresh air. Then, I have breakfast. I eat bread with honey and drink a glass of milk. After breakfast, I go for a walk. I like to walk in the forest. The forest is very green. I see many animals. I see squirrels, rabbits, and sometimes a deer. The animals are very fast. I try to be quiet so I do not scare them.

There is a small school in my village. I go to school from Monday to Friday. There are only twenty students in my school. We all study in one big room. Our teacher is Mrs. Green. She is very patient and she teaches us many things. We learn to read and write. We also learn math and geography. I like my school because my friends are there. We play football in the school yard during the break. We have a lot of fun together.

In the afternoon, I come home and do my homework. After I finish my homework, I help my parents. I water the plants in the garden and feed our chickens. We have ten chickens. They give us fresh eggs every day. I like to collect the eggs in a small basket. My cat, Whiskers, follows me everywhere. He is a very happy cat. He likes to play with a ball of wool.

On Saturdays, there is a small market in the village square. People from other villages come to sell their products. They sell cheese, honey, handmade clothes, and beautiful flowers. My family and I go to the market every Saturday. We buy things we need and talk to our friends. The market is very busy and colorful. I like the smell of the fresh flowers and the honey.

On Sundays, the village is very quiet. Many people go to the small church near the square. After church, families have a big lunch together. We eat chicken and vegetables from our garden. In the afternoon, we go for a long walk near the river. The river is very clean. Some people fish in the river, but I like to watch the water. The sound of the water is very relaxing.

Life in a small village is very simple. We do not have big cinemas or shopping malls. We do not have many cars or noisy streets. But we have nature and good friends. I feel very safe and happy here. I like the change of the seasons. In the spring, everything is green and the flowers open. In the summer, it is warm and we can swim in the river. In the autumn, the leaves on the trees turn red and yellow. In the winter, sometimes it snows and the village looks like a fairy tale.

I love my village very much. It is my home. I want to live here for a long time. People in the city are always in a hurry. They have a lot of stress. But here, life is slow and peaceful. We have time to talk and enjoy the small things. I think everyone should visit a small village sometimes. It is good for the soul. You can see the stars clearly at night because there are no big lights. The sky is very dark and the stars are very bright. It is a beautiful sight.

When I grow up, I want to be a gardener. I want to take care of the plants and the trees. I want to help my village stay beautiful. My parents are proud of me. They say that being happy is the most important thing in life. And I am very happy in my small village. Every day is a gift. I am grateful for the fresh air, the green trees, and my kind neighbors. This is my life, and I love it. I hope my village stays this way forever. It is a special place in the world. Goodnight, my beautiful village. I will see you tomorrow.
