---
title: "The Blue Notebook"
level: "a1"
date: "2026-06-24"
---

Mia has a small blue notebook. It is in her school bag every day. The notebook is old, but Mia likes it very much. It has pictures of stars on the cover.

On Monday morning, Mia goes to school early. The sky is gray, and the street is quiet. She walks with her brother Leo. He has a red coat and a big lunch box.

At school, Mia opens her bag. Her blue notebook is not there. She looks under her books. She looks in her desk. She looks on the floor. The notebook is gone.

Mia feels sad. The notebook has her stories, her drawings, and a photo of her family. She thinks about the bus stop, the street, and the school door.

After class, Mia walks back with Leo. She looks near the gate. She looks beside the tree. Then she sees something blue under a bench. It is her notebook.

The notebook is wet from the rain, but it is not broken. Mia opens it carefully. The family photo is safe. Her stories are safe too.

At home, Mia puts the notebook near the window. The sun comes out, and the pages become dry. That night, Mia writes a new story. It is about a blue notebook that gets lost and comes home again.
