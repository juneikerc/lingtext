---
title: "Why I Love Reading Books"
level: "b1"
date: "2026-05-08"
---

When I was a child, my grandmother gave me a small book about animals. I was six years old, and I could not read very well. However, I loved the pictures, and I asked my grandmother to read the stories to me every night. She used different voices for each animal, and I laughed every time. That small book changed my life, and it is the main reason why I still love reading today. I will never forget those evenings when we sat together under a warm blanket.

Many people say that reading is boring because it takes too much time. They prefer to watch short videos or play games on their phones. Although I understand their point of view, I think they are missing something important. Reading is not just a way to pass the time. It is an opportunity to explore new worlds, learn about different cultures, and understand other people's feelings. In my opinion, a good book can give you emotions that a screen cannot offer.

One of the best things about reading is that it helps you relax. When I have a difficult day at school or work, I sit in a comfortable chair and open a good book. After a few minutes, I forget my problems because the story takes me somewhere else. This does not usually happen when I watch television, because there are too many advertisements and loud noises. A book gives you peace and quiet, and you can choose the speed at which you read. You can stop to think about a sentence, or you can read fast when the story is exciting.

Another advantage is that reading improves your vocabulary. When you read regularly, you meet new words in different contexts. At first, you may not understand all of them, but after a while you start to guess their meaning. If you read books in another language, this effect is even stronger. I started reading simple English stories two years ago, and now I can understand much more than before. In addition, reading helps you write better because you see how sentences are built. You learn grammar naturally without studying boring rules.

Some people argue that books are too expensive and that they take up too much space at home. However, this is not completely true. Public libraries exist in almost every town, and you can borrow books for free. Moreover, there are many websites where you can find affordable digital books that do not need any physical space. If you want to read, money and space are not real problems. The only things you need are time and a little patience.

I also believe that reading makes you more empathetic. When you read about characters who face serious problems, you start to understand their fears and hopes. This is especially true for novels that describe real human situations. Last month, I read a book about a family that moved to a new country without knowing the language. Although their experience was very different from mine, I could feel their sadness and their courage. Because of that book, I now try to be kinder to people who come from other places. I understand that their life is not easy.

In conclusion, reading is one of the most valuable habits a person can have. It entertains you, teaches you new things, and helps you grow as a person. If you have not read a book in a long time, I recommend that you visit a library and choose something that interests you. You may be surprised by how much you enjoy it. After all, every great adventure can start with a single page. I hope that more people will discover the joy of reading and share it with their friends and family.
