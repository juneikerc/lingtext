**Anna:** Hello, Ben.
**Ben:** Hello, Anna. How are you today?
**Anna:** I am fine, thank you. And you?
**Ben:** I am good. I am a little tired.
**Anna:** Why are you tired?
**Ben:** I work a lot today. I work in an office.
**Anna:** Oh, I see. I work in a shop.
**Ben:** What do you sell in the shop?
**Anna:** I sell clothes. I sell shirts, pants, and shoes.
**Ben:** That is nice. Do you like your job?
**Anna:** Yes, I like my job. I like to talk to people.
**Ben:** I like to talk to people too.

**Anna:** Where are you now, Ben?
**Ben:** I am in the city center. I am near a café.
**Anna:** I like that café. The coffee is good.
**Ben:** Yes, the coffee is very good. Do you want to sit?
**Anna:** Yes, I want to sit and talk.
**Ben:** Okay. Let us sit here.

**Anna:** What do you want to drink?
**Ben:** I want a coffee with milk.
**Anna:** I want a tea, please.
**Ben:** The waiter is coming.
**Anna:** Hello.
**Ben:** Hello. Two drinks, please.

**Anna:** Do you live near here?
**Ben:** Yes, I live near here. I live in a small apartment.
**Anna:** Do you live alone?
**Ben:** Yes, I live alone. And you?
**Anna:** I live with my sister.
**Ben:** That is nice. How old is your sister?
**Anna:** She is twenty-five years old.
**Ben:** My sister is twenty-seven years old.

**Anna:** What do you do after work?
**Ben:** After work, I go home. I cook dinner.
**Anna:** Do you like to cook?
**Ben:** Yes, I like to cook simple food.
**Anna:** What food do you cook?
**Ben:** I cook pasta and rice.
**Anna:** I like pasta. Pasta is good.

**Ben:** Do you like music, Anna?
**Anna:** Yes, I like music. I like pop music.
**Ben:** I like pop music too.
**Anna:** Do you play an instrument?
**Ben:** No, I do not play an instrument.
**Anna:** I do not play an instrument either.

**Ben:** What do you do on the weekend?
**Anna:** On the weekend, I rest. I meet my friends.
**Ben:** Where do you meet your friends?
**Anna:** We meet in a park or in a café.
**Ben:** I like the park. The park is quiet.
**Anna:** Yes, the park is quiet and green.

**Ben:** Do you like sports?
**Anna:** Yes, I like sports. I like walking.
**Ben:** I like walking too.
**Anna:** Do you walk in the morning or in the evening?
**Ben:** I walk in the evening.
**Anna:** I walk in the morning.

**Ben:** Do you have a pet?
**Anna:** Yes, I have a cat.
**Ben:** What is your cat’s name?
**Anna:** Her name is Lily.
**Ben:** That is a nice name.
**Anna:** Do you have a pet?
**Ben:** No, I do not have a pet.

**Anna:** What time do you wake up?
**Ben:** I wake up at seven in the morning.
**Anna:** I wake up at six.
**Ben:** Six is early.
**Anna:** Yes, it is early, but I like the morning.

**Ben:** Do you like the city?
**Anna:** Yes, I like the city. There are many shops.
**Ben:** I like the city too. There are many people.
**Anna:** Sometimes the city is loud.
**Ben:** Yes, sometimes it is loud.

**Anna:** What is your favorite place in the city?
**Ben:** My favorite place is the library.
**Anna:** I like the library too. I like to read.
**Ben:** What do you read?
**Anna:** I read simple books.
**Ben:** I read newspapers.

**Anna:** Our drinks are here.
**Ben:** Thank you.
**Anna:** This tea is good.
**Ben:** This coffee is very good.

**Anna:** It is nice to talk with you, Ben.
**Ben:** It is nice to talk with you too, Anna.
**Anna:** We can meet again.
**Ben:** Yes, we can meet again tomorrow.
**Anna:** Tomorrow is good for me.
**Ben:** Great. See you tomorrow.
**Anna:** See you tomorrow.
