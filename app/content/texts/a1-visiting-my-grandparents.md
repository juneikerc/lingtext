---
title: "Visiting My Grandparents"
level: "a1"
date: "2026-04-22"
---

Every Sunday, I visit my grandparents. They live in a small house near the park. I love them very much.

I take the bus at ten o'clock. The ride is short. I look out the window and see trees and shops.

When I arrive, my grandmother opens the door. She smiles and gives me a big hug. "Come in!" she says. "We have food for you."

My grandfather is in the kitchen. He is making soup. The smell is very good. I sit at the table and they ask me about my week.

"How is school?" my grandmother asks.

"It is good," I say. "I learn many new things."

We eat lunch together. We have soup, bread, and fruit. My grandmother makes the best food in the world.

After lunch, we sit in the living room. My grandfather tells me stories about his life. He talks about his work and his travels. I like to listen.

Sometimes we play cards. Sometimes we walk in the park. The air is fresh and the birds sing.

At five o'clock, I must go home. I say goodbye to my grandparents. They give me a bag of fruit to take with me.

"Come back soon," my grandmother says.

"I will," I promise. "I love you both."

I leave with a happy heart. Family is very important. I am glad I can visit my grandparents every week.
