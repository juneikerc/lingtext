title: "Cooking Dinner with My Sister"
level: "a1"
date: "2026-04-08"
---

On Friday evening, I cook dinner with my sister, Ana. We are in the kitchen at 6:30. We want to make pasta and salad.

First, Ana cuts tomatoes and onions. I wash the lettuce and put it in a big bowl. Then, I boil water for the pasta. The kitchen is busy, but it is fun. We talk and laugh together.

Ana adds salt and oil to the salad. I cook the pasta for ten minutes. After that, we put the pasta on two plates. We add tomato sauce and some cheese.

Our parents come home at 7:15. They are tired, but they smile when they see the food. We all sit at the table and eat together.

The pasta is very good. The salad is fresh and tasty. My father says, “Great job!” My sister and I feel very happy.

I like cooking with Ana because we work as a team. Dinner with my family is one of my favorite times of the week.
