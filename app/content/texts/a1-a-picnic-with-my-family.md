title: "A Picnic with My Family"
level: "a1"
date: "2026-05-08"
---

Today is Saturday. The sun is shining and the weather is warm. My family and I go to the park for a picnic. I am very happy because I love picnics.

My mother packs food in a big basket. She puts bread, cheese, apples, and juice inside. My father carries a blanket and a ball. My little sister brings her favorite toy. I help my mother close the basket.

We walk to the park because it is near our house. In the park, we see many people. Some children play games. Some women sit under trees and talk. A man walks his dog near the river. Everything looks nice.

My father finds a good place under a big tree. We put the blanket on the grass and sit down. My mother takes out the food. We eat sandwiches and drink juice. The bread is fresh and the apples are sweet. I eat two apples because they are so good.

After lunch, we play together. My father and I throw the ball. My sister runs after it. She is small, but she runs fast. My mother watches us and smiles. She says we are a happy family.

Later, we walk near the river. We see ducks in the water. My sister gives them some bread. The ducks swim fast and make funny noises. We laugh and take pictures with my mother's phone.

In the evening, we pack our things and go home. I am tired but very happy. I love days like this. My family is kind and fun. I want to have another picnic next week. I think Saturday is the best day of the week.
