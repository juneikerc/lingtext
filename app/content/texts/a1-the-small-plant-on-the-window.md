---
title: "The Small Plant on the Window"
level: "a1"
date: "2026-05-18"
---

I have a small plant. It is on my window. The plant is green. It has five small leaves. I like it very much.

Every morning, I open the window. The sun comes into my room. My plant likes the sun. I give it a little water. I do not give it too much water.

My sister says, "Your plant is very small." I say, "Yes, but it is growing." She smiles and touches one leaf. The leaf is soft.

At school, I tell my teacher about my plant. My teacher says plants need light, water, and time. I think this is true.

After school, I look at my plant again. Is there a new leaf? Today, I see one very small leaf. I am happy.

My plant is not big. It does not have flowers. But it is alive, and it is mine. I take care of it every day.
