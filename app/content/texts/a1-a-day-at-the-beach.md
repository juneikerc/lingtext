---
title: "A Day at the Beach"
level: "a1"
---

Maria and her brother Pedro go to the beach **today**. It is very hot **outside**. The sun is big and yellow in the sky. Maria wears a blue hat. Pedro has a red towel.

**Maria:** Wow! The water looks very nice **today**!

**Pedro:** Yes! I want to swim **now**. Do you want to swim with me?

**Maria:** I do not know. I think the water is cold.

**Pedro:** Come on! It is not cold. It is warm. Touch the water with your foot.

Maria walks to the water. She puts her foot in the sea. The water is nice. It is not too cold and not too hot.

**Maria:** You are right! The water feels good. I want to swim **now**.

**Pedro:** Great! We can swim together.

Maria and Pedro swim for ten minutes. Then they sit on the sand. Pedro opens his bag. He takes out two apples.

**Pedro:** Do you want an apple? I have two.

**Maria:** Yes, please! I am hungry **now**.

**Pedro:** Here you are.

**Maria:** Thank you! This apple is very sweet.

**Pedro:** My mother buys good apples. She knows I like them.

**Maria:** Your mother is very kind. Where is she **today**?

**Pedro:** She is at home. She works **today**.

**Maria:** Oh. Does she like the beach too?

**Pedro:** Yes! She loves the beach. But she cannot come **today**. She has a lot of work.

**Maria:** That is sad. I hope she can come tomorrow.

**Pedro:** Me too. Tomorrow is Sunday. She does not work on Sunday.

**Maria:** Good! We can come together tomorrow.

**Pedro:** Yes! That is a good idea.

A small bird comes near them. It is brown and white. It looks at Maria's apple.

**Maria:** Look! A bird!

**Pedro:** Do not **give** it your apple. It can find food **outside**.

**Maria:** But it looks hungry. Can I **give** it a small piece?

**Pedro:** Okay. A very small piece.

Maria breaks a little piece of apple. She puts it on the sand. The bird eats it quickly. Then it flies away.

**Maria:** It was so cute!

**Pedro:** Birds like the beach too. They live **outside** all the time.

**Maria:** I like animals. Do you have a pet at home?

**Pedro:** Yes! I have a small dog. His name is Coco.

**Maria:** That is a nice name. Is Coco **outside** now?

**Pedro:** No. He is at home with my mother.

**Maria:** I want to meet Coco someday.

**Pedro:** You can come to my house tomorrow. After the beach.

**Maria:** Really? Thank you!

**Pedro:** We can play with Coco. He likes new friends.

**Maria:** I think dogs are the best animals.

**Pedro:** I think so too. But birds are nice too.

**Maria:** All animals are nice. Even the small ones.

The sun is very hot **now**. Maria puts on her hat. Pedro puts on his sunglasses.

**Pedro:** It is time to swim again. The water will feel good.

**Maria:** Yes! Let us go!

They run to the water. The waves are small **today**. The water is clear and blue. They swim and laugh.

**Maria:** This is the best day!

**Pedro:** I think **today** is perfect.

**Maria:** Me too. I love the beach.

**Pedro:** And I love swimming with you.

**Maria:** You are a good brother.

**Pedro:** And you are a good sister.

They swim for one hour. Then they dry themselves with Pedro's red towel. They sit and watch the sea.

**Maria:** Look at the sky. It is so blue.

**Pedro:** No clouds **today**. Just sun.

**Maria:** I want to come here every day.

**Pedro:** Me too. But we have school tomorrow.

**Maria:** Oh yes. School.

**Pedro:** Do not worry. We have **today**. We can enjoy it.

**Maria:** Yes! Let us enjoy **today**.

**Pedro:** Let us walk on the sand. We can find shells.

**Maria:** Good idea! I want to take a shell home.

They walk along the beach. They find many shells. Some are white. Some are pink. Maria finds a very big one.

**Maria:** Look at this one! It is so big!

**Pedro:** That is a good shell. You can **give** it to Mom.

**Maria:** Yes! She will like it.

**Pedro:** Let us go home **now**. It is getting **late**.

**Maria:** Okay. I am tired but happy.

**Pedro:** Me too. Today was a great day.

**Maria:** The best day at the beach!

**Pedro:** Let us come back soon.

**Maria:** Yes! Next weekend?

**Pedro:** Next weekend. I promise.

They pack their things. They walk home together. The sun is still warm on their backs.
