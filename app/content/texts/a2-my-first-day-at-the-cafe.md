---
title: "My First Day at the Cafe"
level: "a2"
date: "2026-05-18"
---

Last Monday, I started a small job at a cafe near my house. I was nervous because it was my first job. The cafe is not very big, but many people come in the morning. They buy coffee, tea, bread, and small cakes before they go to work.

First, the manager showed me the kitchen. She explained where the cups, plates, and spoons were. Then she showed me how to clean the tables. It was easy, but I had to work fast. When one customer left, another customer often came in two minutes later.

At ten o'clock, a man asked me for a coffee with milk. I made a mistake and gave him tea. I felt bad, but the man was kind. He smiled and said, "It is okay. Everyone has a first day." My manager helped me make the right drink. After that, I checked every order carefully.

During my break, I sat near the window and ate a sandwich. I watched people walking outside. Some people looked tired. Some people talked on their phones. A woman came in with a small child, and the child wanted a chocolate cake. He looked very happy when his mother bought it.

In the afternoon, I felt more comfortable. I cleaned tables, carried plates, and said hello to customers. I also learned the names of two other workers. One is Daniel, and the other is Mia. They told me that the job is busy, but it can be fun.

When I went home, my feet hurt, but I was proud. I earned my first money, and I learned many useful things. Next week, I will work there again. I think the second day will be easier because I know what to do now.
