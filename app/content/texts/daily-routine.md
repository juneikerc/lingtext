title: "My Daily Routine"
level: "a1"
---

Every day, I wake up at 7 o'clock. I wash my face and brush my teeth. Then, I have breakfast with my family. I usually eat bread and drink orange juice. My father drinks coffee, and my mother drinks tea. We talk about our day. At 8 o'clock, I go to school. I like my teachers and my friends. We study English and math. I have many friends at school. My best friend is Sam. We play together.

In the morning, I have four classes. First, I have English class. I like English because it is interesting. My teacher is very kind. She helps me with new words. Second, I have math class. Math is sometimes difficult, but I try my best. Third, I have science class. We learn about plants and animals. Fourth, I have history class. We learn about the past. At 12 o'clock, I have lunch. I eat at the school cafeteria. I usually have a sandwich and an apple. I talk with my friends during lunch. We laugh and have fun.

In the afternoon, I have two more classes. I have music class and art class. I like music because I love to sing. I like art because I like to draw pictures. After school, at 3 o'clock, I go home. I walk home with my friends. We see many things on the street. We see cars, buses, and shops. When I get home, I see my cat. My cat's name is Luna. She is very cute and soft. I play with Luna for some time. Then, I do my homework. I do my math homework first, then my English homework. Homework is important because it helps me learn.

At 5 o'clock, my parents come home from work. My father works in an office, and my mother works in a hospital. They are tired, but they are happy to see me. We help each other. I help my mother in the kitchen. We cook dinner together. Today, we are cooking chicken and rice. It smells very good. My father sets the table. He puts the plates, spoons, and glasses on the table. We sit together and eat. We talk about what happened at school and at work. My mother tells a funny story. We all laugh.

After dinner, I help clean the table. I wash the dishes, and my father dries them. Then, I have some free time. In the evening, I watch TV or read a book. I like to watch cartoons on TV. They are very funny. Sometimes, I read a book about space. Space is very big and beautiful. I want to be an astronaut one day. At 8 o'clock, I take a shower. I use warm water and soap. I feel very clean and relaxed. Then, I put on my pajamas. My pajamas are blue and very comfortable.

Before I go to bed, I talk to my parents. I say goodnight to them. I also say goodnight to Luna. She is sleeping on the sofa. I go to my bedroom. My bedroom is small but cozy. I have a bed, a desk, and a lamp. I turn on the lamp and read for ten minutes. Then, I turn off the light. I go to bed at 9:30. I close my eyes and think about my day. I am happy. Tomorrow is a new day. I want to learn more and see my friends.

On the weekend, my routine is different. I do not go to school on Saturday and Sunday. I wake up late, at 9 o'clock. I have a big breakfast. We eat pancakes and eggs. On Saturday, I go to the park with my family. We play football and run. The park is very green. I see many people and dogs. On Sunday, I visit my grandparents. They live in a small house with a garden. My grandmother makes delicious cookies. I love my grandparents very much. My daily routine is simple, but I like it. Every day is special. I learn new things, I see people I love, and I am healthy. This is my life, and I am grateful for it. Having a routine helps me stay organized and happy. It makes me feel safe and prepared for the day. I hope everyone has a good daily routine too. Everyday life can be wonderful if you enjoy the small things. Breakfast with family, learning at school, playing with a pet, and sleeping in a warm bed are all great things. I am a student, and I am growing every day. My routine will change as I get older, but I will always remember these happy days. Now, it is time to sleep. Goodnight world.
