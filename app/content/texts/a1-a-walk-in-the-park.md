---
title: "A Walk in the Park"
level: "a1"
date: "2026-04-12"
---

Tom and his sister Lily go to the park. It is Sunday afternoon. The sky is blue and clear.

**Tom:** Do you want to walk or sit?

**Lily:** Let us walk first. I want to see the flowers.

They walk on a small path. There are trees on both sides. The leaves are green.

**Tom:** Look at that bird!

**Lily:** It is very small. And very fast!

The bird flies from one tree to another. It is yellow and black.

**Tom:** I think it is looking for food.

**Lily:** Birds like this park. There are many trees.

They continue walking. Soon they see a big open area. Some children are playing with a ball.

**Lily:** The park is full of people today.

**Tom:** Yes. Everyone likes sunny days.

They find a bench near a small lake. They sit down.

**Lily:** The water looks nice.

**Tom:** Do you see the ducks? They are swimming.

**Lily:** Yes! There are five ducks. Three are white and two are brown.

**Tom:** They look happy in the water.

**Lily:** Do you have any bread? We can feed them.

**Tom:** No. I forgot to bring some.

**Lily:** Next time, we should bring bread for the ducks.

**Tom:** Good idea.

An old man walks by. He has a dog on a leash. The dog is big and friendly.

**Old Man:** Good afternoon.

**Tom and Lily:** Good afternoon!

The dog wags its tail. Lily smiles.

**Lily:** Your dog is very cute.

**Old Man:** Thank you. His name is Max.

**Lily:** Hello, Max!

The dog sits down. The old man gives him a small treat.

**Old Man:** We come here every day. Max loves this park.

**Tom:** It is a beautiful place.

**Old Man:** Yes. I have been coming here for twenty years.

The old man and Max continue walking. Tom and Lily watch them leave.

**Lily:** I want a dog someday.

**Tom:** Maybe when we have a bigger house.

**Lily:** Yes. With a garden.

They sit quietly for a few minutes. A soft wind blows. It feels good.

**Tom:** Do you hear that?

**Lily:** What?

**Tom:** Music. Someone is playing a guitar.

They look around. Near the trees, a young man is sitting on a blanket. He is playing a guitar and singing.

**Lily:** Let us go listen.

They walk closer. The music is slow and beautiful. Some people are sitting on the grass, listening.

**Tom:** He plays very well.

**Lily:** Yes. And his voice is nice too.

They sit on the grass nearby. The man sings a song about the sun and the sea.

**Lily:** This is so relaxing.

**Tom:** The park is full of surprises.

When the song ends, people clap. The man smiles and starts another song.

**Lily:** I am glad we came today.

**Tom:** Me too. It is better than staying at home.

After three songs, Tom and Lily get up. They want to walk more.

**Tom:** Thank you for the music!

The guitar player nods and smiles.

They walk to the other side of the park. There is a small playground. Some children are climbing and sliding.

**Lily:** I remember playing here when I was little.

**Tom:** Yes. You loved the swings.

**Lily:** I still do!

They both laugh.

**Tom:** Do you want to go home soon?

**Lily:** Not yet. Let us walk around the lake one more time.

**Tom:** Okay. The sun is still high.

They walk slowly around the lake. The water is calm. Some leaves float on the surface.

**Lily:** Look! A fish!

**Tom:** Where?

**Lily:** There! Near that big rock.

Tom looks. He sees a small orange fish swimming near the rock.

**Tom:** You have good eyes!

**Lily:** I always look at the water. You never know what you will see.

They finish walking around the lake. The sun starts to go down. The sky becomes orange and pink.

**Tom:** It is getting late.

**Lily:** Yes. We should go home.

**Tom:** Did you have a good time?

**Lily:** Yes! The park was beautiful today. The flowers, the music, the ducks...

**Tom:** And the fish!

**Lily:** Yes! And the fish!

They walk toward the exit. At the gate, they stop and look back.

**Tom:** Same time next week?

**Lily:** Definitely. I love our Sunday walks.

They hold hands and walk home together. It was a perfect afternoon.
