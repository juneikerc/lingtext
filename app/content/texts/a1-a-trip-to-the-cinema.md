# A Trip to the Cinema

Lucy and Ben want to see a new film. It is Saturday afternoon. The sun is shining.

**Lucy:** Do you want to go to the cinema?

**Ben:** Yes! I want to see the new cartoon about a robot.

**Lucy:** Great! Let us go now.

They walk to the cinema. It is near the park. The building is big and red.

**Ben:** Look at the posters!

There are many posters on the wall. One poster shows a blue robot with big eyes.

**Lucy:** That is our film!

They go inside. A woman is behind the counter.

**Woman:** Hello! How can I help you?

**Lucy:** Two tickets for the robot film, please.

**Woman:** That is twelve dollars.

Lucy gives the money. The woman gives them two tickets.

**Woman:** Your film starts in ten minutes. Room three.

**Ben:** Thank you!

They walk to the snack bar. Ben sees popcorn and candy.

**Ben:** Can we have popcorn?

**Lucy:** Yes. A small popcorn, please.

The man gives them a yellow box of popcorn. It smells very good.

**Ben:** I love popcorn!

They go to Room Three. It is dark inside. Many people are already sitting down.

**Lucy:** Let us sit here.

They sit in the middle. The chairs are soft. Ben eats some popcorn.

**Ben:** This is exciting!

The lights go down. The screen is very big and bright.

The film starts. It is about a small robot who wants to be a real friend. The robot is funny and kind.

**Ben:** He is so cute!

**Lucy:** Yes. And he is brave too.

The robot helps a lost dog find its home. People in the cinema laugh.

**Ben:** The dog is funny!

**Lucy:** Shh! But yes, it is funny.

Later, the robot is sad. He sits alone in the rain.

**Ben:** Oh no! I am sad too.

**Lucy:** Do not worry. It will be okay.

At the end, the robot finds a family. Everyone is happy. People in the cinema clap.

**Ben:** What a good film!

**Lucy:** I liked it very much.

They walk outside. The sun is going down. The sky is pink and orange.

**Ben:** Can we buy the robot toy?

**Lucy:** Maybe next week.

**Ben:** Okay. Today was perfect.

**Lucy:** Yes. A perfect Saturday.

They walk home together. Ben talks about the robot all the way.
