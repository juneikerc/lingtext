"I **came** **straight** from my apartment to here," Ana **explained**. "I **didn't** **stop** anywhere. I just **walked** along Main **Street** and then **turned** onto Oak **Avenue**."

"No, **dear**," the woman **said** **kindly**. "But I'll **keep** my **eyes** **open** for you."
