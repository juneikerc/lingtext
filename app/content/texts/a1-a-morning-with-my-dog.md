--- 
title: "A Morning with My Dog" 
level: "a1" 
date: "2026-04-08"
--- 

My dog is small and brown. His name is Max. Every morning, I wake up at 7:00. Max is very happy to see me. He jumps on my bed and moves his tail.

First, I wash my face and get dressed. Then, I take Max outside. We walk on the street near my house. The air is fresh, and the sun is warm. Max likes to smell the flowers and run after birds.

After the walk, I give Max water and food. He eats in the kitchen. I make my breakfast. I usually eat bread and drink milk. Max sits next to me and looks at me with big eyes.

At 8:00, I sit at my desk and read a book or check my phone. Max sleeps on the floor. He is quiet and sweet.

I like my mornings with Max. They are simple, calm, and happy.
